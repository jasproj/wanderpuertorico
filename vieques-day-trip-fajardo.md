## Getting There: Ferry vs Flight vs Tour

### Public Ferry (Cheapest, Least Reliable)
- **Cost:** $4 each way
- **Time:** 1.5 hours from Ceiba
- **Problem:** Tickets sell out instantly online. Standby lines start at 4am. Ferries cancel without warning.
- **Verdict:** Only if you're flexible and lucky.

### Puddle Jumper Flight (Fast, Pricey)
- **Cost:** $80–150 round trip
- **Time:** 15 minutes from Ceiba or San Juan
- **Operators:** Vieques Air Link, Cape Air
- **Verdict:** Best for day trips. Guarantees your schedule.

### Boat Tour from Fajardo (Easiest)
- **Cost:** $150–250 per person
- **Time:** ~1 hour each way by powerboat
- **Includes:** Transport, snorkeling, beach time, lunch
- **Verdict:** Best value for day-trippers. No logistics to manage.

## What to Do on a Vieques Day Trip

### Option A: Beach + Snorkel Focus

**Morning:** Arrive via flight or tour boat.

**Midday:** Hit the beaches:
- **Sun Bay** — Easiest to access, calm water, facilities
- **Media Luna** — Crescent-shaped, great snorkeling
- **Playa Negra** — Black sand, fewer people

**Afternoon:** Snorkel at Blue Beach or explore the old military bunkers on the wildlife refuge.

**Evening:** Catch your flight/boat back.

---

### Option B: Bio Bay Focus (Overnight Required)

Here's the catch: **Mosquito Bay tours run after dark.** If the bio bay is your priority, you'll need to stay overnight or time a late return flight.

**Why it's worth it:** Mosquito Bay holds the Guinness World Record for brightest bioluminescence. Fajardo's Laguna Grande is convenient, but Vieques glows 5x brighter.

## Pro Tips

1. **Skip the ferry** — Seriously. The stress isn't worth $4 savings.
2. **Bring cash** — Some Vieques vendors don't take cards.
3. **Rent a golf cart** — If flying in solo, this is how locals get around.
4. **Book bio bay separately** — If you're staying overnight, book the Mosquito Bay tour directly with a Vieques operator.
5. **Check the moon phase** — Bio bay tours run regardless, but new moon = best glow.

## Is a Day Trip Worth It?

**Yes, if:**
- You want to snorkel and beach without overnight costs
- Ferry logistics stress you out
- You're combining with a Fajardo bio bay tour that evening

**Consider overnight if:**
- Mosquito Bay bio bay is your main goal
- You want to explore at your own pace
- You love off-the-grid vibes
