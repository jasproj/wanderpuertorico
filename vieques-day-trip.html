Vieques Day Trip from Fajardo: Complete Guide (2025)

Updated December 2025 • 7 min read

Vieques sits 8 miles off Puerto Rico's east coast—a laid-back island with wild horses, empty beaches, and the brightest bioluminescent bay on Earth. Most visitors stay overnight, but a well-planned day trip lets you hit the highlights without the ferry lottery stress.

Getting There: Ferry vs Flight vs Tour

Public Ferry (Cheapest, Least Reliable)

Cost: $4 each way | Time: 1.5 hours from Ceiba

Problem: Tickets sell out instantly online. Standby lines start at 4am. Ferries cancel without warning.

Puddle Jumper Flight (Fast, Pricey)

Cost: $80–150 round trip | Time: 15 minutes

Verdict: Best for day trips. Guarantees your schedule.

Boat Tour from Fajardo (Easiest)

Cost: $150–250 per person | Time: ~1 hour each way

Includes: Transport, snorkeling, beach time, lunch

Verdict: Best value for day-trippers. No logistics to manage.

Pro Tips

  1. Skip the ferry — Seriously. The stress isn't worth $4 savings.
  2. Bring cash — Some Vieques vendors don't take cards.
  3. Rent a golf cart — If flying in solo, this is how locals get around.
  4. Check the moon phase — Bio bay is brightest during new moon.
